A brief overview of my professional background and interests.

    I currently work as a Cyber Security Analyst at an MSSP (Managed Security Service Provider), where I focus on protecting organisations from emerging threats and vulnerabilities. Prior to this, I gained extensive experience in computer hardware and technical support, specialising in system architecture, component integration, and hardware diagnostics.

  • Digital Privacy

    As a strong advocate for digital privacy, I believe it's essential to protect personal data in today's interconnected world.

    As a Linux enthusiast, I run Linux distributions on all my personal machines and am a strong advocate for FOSS (Free and Open Source Software). I believe that open source allows us to create more secure, transparent, and user-controlled computing environments. Currently, I am running Arch Linux on my personal machines, with Hyprland as my window manager of choice.

  • Homelab

    I maintain a homelab where I experiment with self-hosting privacy-respecting software. This hands-on approach allows me to stay current with emerging technologies while maintaining control over my digital infrastructure.
