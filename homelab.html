Homelab

My self-hosted infrastructure and services running on a Synology NAS.

  • Hardware

    Synology DS224+
    • CPU: Intel Celeron J4125 (4 cores, 2GHz)
    • RAM: 18 GB (upgraded from stock 2 GB)
    • Storage: 8 TB

  • Services

    Jellyfin - A free and open-source media system that allows you to stream your own media content, offering a self-hosted alternative to services like Netflix.

    Synology Photos - A comprehensive photo management solution that helps you organise, share, and protect your photos.

    Syncthing - A continuous file synchronisation program that synchronises files between two or more computers in real time.

    Paperless-ngx - A document management system that transforms your physical documents into a searchable online archive.

    Watcharr - A self-hosted watchlist application for tracking TV shows and movies.

    Home Assistant - An open-source home automation platform that puts local control and privacy first.

    Actual Budget - A personal finance tool that helps you track your spending and manage your budget effectively.
